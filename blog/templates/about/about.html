Himalyan Health Project

Over the course of a week, working from 8 am to 6 pm. I was based in Mahabodhi Hospital, Leh, Ladakh as a voluntary dentist assistant and coordinator. Being based in the dentistry sector, my main responsibility was to make sure that the patients moved through check-ups, scaling/cleaning, and surgery efficiently. The language barrier, a patient’s unique aural problems, and managing appointments were the main issues we faced. Being able to communicate in Hindi, I helped translate and crossed the language barrier. I also applied my knowledge of computing and physics to fix lots of equipment. My job was to aid the main dentist with scribing as she carried out the check-ups. I was pro- active and thought ahead by planning appointments based on what I was scribing so that we saved time. I also assisted another dentist with scaling and cleaning which was a new experience. I shadowed another doctor in the surgery which involved me preparing and equipment and dental instruments. I learned how to deal with a large amount of stress in different situations as well as understanding a lot about oral hygiene.
